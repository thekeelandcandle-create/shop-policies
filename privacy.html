When you visit our Site, we may automatically collect certain technical information including your IP address, browser type and version, operating system, referring URLs, pages viewed, and the date and time of your visit. This information is used solely for analytics and site improvement purposes and is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Our application uses the TikTok Content Posting API solely to publish product-related videos and images to our official TikTok business account (@thekeelandcandle).
