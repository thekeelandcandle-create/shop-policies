Our Story

About The Keel & Candle

The Keel & Candle Shop was born from a love of dark fantasy, grimdark fiction, and the kind of literature that leaves marks on you. We make apparel for readers, dungeon crawlers, romantics who prefer their love interests morally compromised, and everyone who has ever stayed up too late because the book demanded it.

Every design is built around a line, an idea, or a feeling that deserves to exist on something you can wear. We print on demand through Printify, ship worldwide, and take our craft as seriously as the genres we love.

Find us on Etsy, follow us on TikTok, and wear the words that live rent-free in your head.
